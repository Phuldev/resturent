About Us

We have been proving happiness to people through food and beverages.
We are known as "Green Farm Meat NSW Halal" located at Wentworthville and Dee Why, NSW. We have come a long way since 2002 in processing the best quality halal meat for our valuable customers, satisfying their need and supplying processed Chicken, Lamb, Beef, Goat and offering the vast variety of BBQ products.

Refer to our core competency is all about buying of healthy live stock followed by in-house slaughtering. We strive to keep setting a new benchmark in meat industry where we are committed to deliver fine quality products in the market. We have online meat purchase facility where you can choose best out of different variety of chicken, lamb, goat and beef.

You simply need to visit our online meat store and place your valuable order with us. The delivery of products will be made with immediate effect.

We are bound and abide by to our motto "Customer First" while offering our products to customer end. Our dedication and endeavour to exceeding all expectations will be paramount.

We give you our meat products in every cut imaginable. We give you the best out of high-quality meat at decent price at our meat shop. Excellent customer service is one which we are focused about the most. At Green Farm Meat NSW Halal we ensure every meat delivered to our customer should have the highest quality which is fresh, healthy and juicy meat.
